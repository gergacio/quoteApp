The Master Peter Deunov

Pope John XXIII called him the greatest philosopher living on the earth at that time.

The Austrian mystic and philosopher Rudolf Steiner called him a great spiritual initiate.

The philosopher and writer Jiddu Krishnamurti called him a World Master.

Albert Einstein said about him “All the world renders homage to me but I render homage to the Master”

The Master gave the knowledge of the coming of a new culture based upon three principles: Love that brings life, Wisdom that gives light and Truth that gives freedom.
